About Helsak Enterprises Limited

HELSAK ENTERPRISES LIMITED is a registered Tanzanian company established on 13th February 2013, specializing in Telecommunication, ICT and Electronic Security Systems.

With over 10 years of industry experience and more than 80 successfully completed projects, we provide design, supply, installation, commissioning and maintenance services for both public and private sector clients.

Our Mission

To satisfy our customers by delivering reliable, secure and innovative technology solutions — even if it requires working beyond expectations.
